# Whittle Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

## Data We Collect
Whittle stores creative session history, proof files (photos, audio, video, 
and text excerpts), and usage counts locally on your device. This data is 
optionally backed up to your personal iCloud account using Apple's iCloud 
Key-Value Store so it can be restored if you reinstall the app.

## Subscriptions
Whittle Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect 
or store your payment information.

## Notifications
If you enable reminders, Whittle sends local push notifications to your device. 
No notification data leaves your device.

## Third Parties
We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. All data 
remains on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

## Data Deletion
To delete all your data, delete the app from your device. To also remove iCloud 
backup data, go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Whittle.
